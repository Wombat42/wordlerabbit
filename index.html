Eliminators

Click on the letters and they will cycle through the same colors as regular wordle. When yellow, the letter is in the word, but not in that spot. When green, the letter is in the word and at that spot.

Click done after updating the letters.

Click edit to type a replacement word.

Click cancel if the app rejects the word. There are a lot of words in the list and some words are not in the app's list.

Click on a word in the Matches section to use it. You can only do this if you haven't clicked on one of the letters.

Use Eliminators to get rid of as many letters as possible. It's best when you have many words that are all equally possible. Say you want to solve a word ending in atch. You could get matches for:

  • batch
  • catch
  • hatch
  • latch
  • match
  • natch
  • patch
  • ratch
  • watch
Your eliminator word may be prowl which would get rid of patch, ratch, watch, and latch. It could also uncover one as the solution.
